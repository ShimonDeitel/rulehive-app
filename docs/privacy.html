Privacy Policy

Last updated July 19, 2026

Rulehive is a board-game rulebook OCR and search app. This policy explains, plainly, what the app does with your camera, your photos, and any data it touches.

Rulebook page photos

When you photograph a rulebook page, that photo is sent to a stateless AI inference relay operated by the developer (a Cloudflare Worker in front of Cloudflare Workers AI) solely to transcribe the visible text. That relay does not require an account, does not attach the image to any identity, and does not retain the photo after the transcription response is returned. Rulehive does not control what any upstream AI infrastructure provider logs at the transport level for abuse prevention, but no persistent copy of your rulebook photos is kept by Rulehive's own backend.

What gets stored, and where

The transcribed text, the page label you're on, and a small on-device thumbnail of each photo are stored locally on your device only, using Apple's on-device SwiftData storage. This index is never uploaded anywhere. Deleting the app, or using "Erase All Data" in Settings, permanently removes it.

Search

Searching your indexed pages happens entirely on your device — typed queries are never sent anywhere, matched only against the text already stored locally.

What Rulehive does not do

  • No account or sign-in of any kind.
  • No advertising and no ad SDKs.
  • No third-party analytics or behavioral tracking.
  • No sale or sharing of your photos or data with any third party for their own purposes.

Subscriptions & payment

Rulehive Pro is an auto-renewable subscription billed through your Apple ID. Payment, billing, and cancellation are handled entirely by Apple under its own App Store terms and privacy practices — Rulehive never receives your payment details.

Children

Rulehive is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Changes

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will change and the new policy will be posted at this same address.
